About

N-Dex is a website that allows users to create collections of cardsets for a variety of uses. Whether you're the active student or someone trying to prepare for the next interview or speech, N-Dex allows you to keep your index cards organized and save you paper. Having access to the internet means you now have access to all of your study-resources whether your at home or across the world. So sign up and start making some cards - it's that simple.
